Are you a student interested in doing a master thesis on deep learning? Feel free to reach out — I always take time to discuss ideas. Some topic areas:

- Federated and decentralized learning
- Out-of-distribution generalization
- Deep learning for climate and sustainability
- Deep learning for understanding social issues
